About Us

Bulls and bears, a Finance Club of VIT promotes finance related activities and
careers through the VIT community. The main objective of this club is to spread
awareness in the domain of finance, in particular investment banking, stocks
and trading, private wealth management and many more. The club provides a
constructive platform for helping the finance aspirants apply theoretical
concepts they have learnt in real life.
